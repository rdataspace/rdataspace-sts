Missions

Flight Profile Payloads Technical

A space shuttle 'mission' began with its launch from earth and included its work in orbit and return to Earth. Due to the shuttle's versatility, mission objectives for each flight varied greatly.

The table below lists all the shuttle missions in chronological order and shows the type of mission and the primary payloads. Mission Types are used as categories in the "Payloads" page, which also gives more details of the primary payloads.
